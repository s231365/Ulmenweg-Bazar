Who we are

We are a group of students developing this web store as part of our university course of Web Engineering. Our aim is to provide a seamless online shopping experience while learning and applying our knowledge in web development. We believe in learning by doing, and this project is a testament to that belief. We hope you enjoy using our web store as much as we enjoyed building it.
